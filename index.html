Why Tokyo?

When you hear "Tokyo", what do you think of? You probably answered with something like Godzilla or Sonic the Hedgehog, maybe you think of Buddhist monks and temples, or maybe you just think of your favorite food. All of these are good examples of why everyone loves Tokyo, but when I hear Tokyo I think of the first high-speed bullet train, I think of automation, and I think of artificial intelligence. Tokyo is the leader in technological innovations and they constantly push the boundaries when it comes to using technology for the betterment of life.

Cost to travel

How much money will you need for your trip to Tokyo? According to Budget Your Trip, you should plan to spend around $172 per day on your vacation in Tokyo, which is the average daily price based on the expenses of other visitors. Past travelers have spent, on average, $41 on meals for one day and $21 on local transportation. Also, the average hotel price in Tokyo for a couple is $162. So, a trip to Tokyo for two people for one week costs on average $2,404. All of these average travel prices have been collected from other travelers to help you plan your own travel budget.
